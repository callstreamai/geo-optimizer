Frequently Asked Questions

What is a GEO scanner?

A GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) scanner analyzes your website to determine how well it performs in AI-generated search results from platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Unlike traditional SEO tools that measure keywords and backlinks, a GEO scanner measures clarity, coverage, structure, and extractability.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Completely free. No login, no email, no credit card. We built this as a resource for businesses to understand how AI sees their website. If you want help implementing the recommendations, our team at Call Stream AI can assist — but the scanner itself is unlimited and free.

How does GEO differ from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for search engine crawlers using keywords, backlinks, and meta tags. GEO optimizes for large language models by focusing on entity clarity, direct answer formatting, structured data, FAQ coverage, and content extractability — the factors that determine whether AI cites your content in its responses.

What does the scanner analyze?

10 categories: Entity Clarity (15%), FAQ Coverage (20%), Content Clarity (15%), Page Structure (10%), Internal Linking (10%), Structured Data (10%), Technical Accessibility (10%), Trust & Authority (10%), plus bonus categories for Scenarios & Context and AI Answer Optimization.
